Title: pesto pasta
Time: 15
Calories: 200
Serves: 2

**INGREDIENTS START**
pasta - 200 g
pesto - 2 tbsp
**INGREDIENTS END**

**TAGS START**
light
fresh
italian
**TAGS END**

**METHOD START**
Bring a pot of salted water to the boil
Add pasta and cook for the length of time on the packet, stirring at the beginning to stop pasta from sticking to itself
Take the pot off the heat
Drain pasta
Return the pasta to the pot
Add the pesto and mix until all of the pasta is coated
Season with salt and pepper to taste
Enjoy!
