Title: toast
Time: 5
Calories: 100
Serves: 2

**INGREDIENTS START**
bread - 4 slices
butter - 20 g 
**INGREDIENTS END**

**TAGS START**
crunchy
simple
**TAGS END**

**METHOD START**
Take bread from bag
Put bread in toaster
Wait for toaster to pop
Butter toast while still hot
Enjoy!
